## 📱 For Android - 2 Simple Options:

### Option A: Web App (5 minutes, no downloads)

1. **Go to:** https://netlify.com (or https://vercel.com)
2. **Sign up** (free, takes 1 minute)
3. **Click:** "Deploy manually" or "Add new site"
4. **Drag and drop:** The folder `stack-family-finance\dist`
5. **Wait:** 30 seconds
6. **You get a URL** like: `https://your-app.netlify.app`
7. **On your Android phone:**
   - Open the URL in Chrome
   - Menu (⋮) → "Add to Home Screen"
   - **Done!** Works like native app!

**Total time: 5 minutes**

---

### Option B: Native APK (30 minutes, needs download)

1. **Download Android Studio:** https://developer.android.com/studio
2. **Install it** (15 minutes, ~10GB)
3. **Open Android Studio**
4. **Click:** "Open"
5. **Select:** `stack-family-finance\android` folder
6. **Wait:** For Gradle sync (2 minutes)
7. **Menu:** Build → Build Bundle(s) / APK(s) → Build APK(s)
8. **Wait:** 5 minutes
9. **Click:** "locate" in notification
10. **Copy APK to phone** and install

**Total time: 30 minutes**
